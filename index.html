My YCDI Metaverse Project

September 27, 2022 by Kareem Ellis

During my tenure as a change agent at Youth Can Do IT (YCDI) I was tasked with designing and modeling a metaverse space. The purpose of the space was to house the annual event hosted by YCDI called #iamWITy or I am a Woman in Technology which empowers young women to enter the tech industry.
Despite learning how to do it from scratch, I created a space using for Mozilla Hubs using a platform called Mozilla Spoke. This allowed for people to join the event through several platforms which includes mobile, laptop or desktop and of course on virtual reality headsets such as the Meta Quest 2.

I completed my first internship

September 27, 2022 by Kareem Ellis

During the summer of 2022, I landed an internship position as a developer at a startup company called One-on-One Educational Services Limited. This internship made use of my current level of Web Development knowledge. I learned about and used cypress, a Javascript End to End Testing Framework to write automated tests for their websites.
At the end of the internship I was granted a contract as a part time contractor to to continue testing their platforms. I am currently working as a contractor there today.
